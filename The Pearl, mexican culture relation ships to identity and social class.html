The pearl and relations to identity/social class.

The book The Pearl by Jhon Stienbeck is related to social class because it is about the story of a Father, a Mother and a baby boy
The family is a lower class family, they do not have very much money. They live in brush houses in a lower class town with more poor people
This shows social class becuase the people are all poor and are low class people. This shows Identity becuase these people are identified by
not caring about money and loving their family.
