Type Of Microorganism

the type of organism that causes the disease of cholera is "Vibrio Bacteria".
the scientific name of the disease is Vibrio Cholerea.

DNA versus RNA diseases

There are two different types of viruses, DNA viruses and RNA viruses.
DNA viruses always have pretty much the same shapre because the DNA does not change.
RNA viruses are the exact opposite, they are unstable and change all the time.
this makes RNA viruses very hard to make vaccinicnes for them.
they change because a RNA strand is different then the DNA strand.
Cholera is a DNA virus.

Pathology(what system does cholera affect?)

Cholera mostly affects the digestive system. it causes infections that produce
diarreha, which empties a lot of water out of the body system causing massive
dehydration and sometimes, in bad cases, death.

Symptoms

there are several symptoms of cholera, dirrhea in massive amounts a time.
Dehydration to point where it is hard to get up and do anything (the dehydration
is caused by the diarrhea because the diarrhea empties all of the water out of
your body). The final sympton is nausea and vomiting.

Transmission

the easiest places to get cholera are in sea water or by eating raw shell fish.
this is one reason why you should always cook shell fish. if you want to prevent
cholera then always cook shellfish, or, just don't eat them at all.

Prognosis of cholera

there are very few cases of death with the diesaseo of cholera.
about 0.1% of people who get cholera die from it. Most of the people that
die from it do not have the most stable immune systems. Another tip to aboid
cholera is to keep your immune system up, strong and running.

Prevention steps

  1. make sure to wash your hands with soap all of the time
  2. drink only safe, clean and filtered water, do not drink ocean salt water.
  3. eat food that is completely cooked, not raw food, this applies especially to fish, mostly shellfish
  4. there is a cholera vaccine, if you do chose to get vaccinated, you may do so, this may help prevent cholera.

Treatment for cholera

cholera is treated by antibiotics and iv fluids. The antibiotics
are used to kill the cholera virus and the iv fluids are used to replace
lost bodily fluids that may have been lost due to the diarreha.

Annual Incidence

there are several outbreaks of cholera going on in countries in the world
Haiti and Yemen just to name a few. There have benn about 4.0 million cases world wide.
there have also been about 143000 deaths worldwide.
