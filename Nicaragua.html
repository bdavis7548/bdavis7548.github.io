History

by the year 1500 BC, Nicaragua was broadly settled(there were lots of people living there).
Nicaraguan settlers started to farm and plant around 450 BC, They mostly grew corn, yucca and beans,  but some other plants were also starting to get introduced around this time.
By the year AD 300, trading partners as far away as modern-day Colombia  and the United States were providing new technology for Nicaragua.
The famous conquistador who arrived in Nicaragua was named Gil González de aliva,
he first arrived in Nicaragua(on the pacific coast) around 1522 and
by the year 1633 the first European settlement was founded in Nicaragua.
in the year 1821, Nicaragua won freedom from spain.
Liberal General José Santos Zelaya took power in the year 1893 and.
by the year 1914 the Bryan-Chamorro Treaty was signed.
On December 23, 1972, A 6.3 earth quake hit Nicaragua in the early morning.

Sports

The national sport of Nicaragua is baseball and one of the more popular sports is fishing.
There are a lot of water sports played on the coasts of Nicaragua, such as surfing or wake boarding. volcano boarding is another sport that is played in Nicaragua, volcano boarding is usually done a laminated board with handles on the front and back.
The name of this sport basically tells you what it is. Volcano boarding is when you board donw the slopes of a volcano.
a metal board for volcano boarding was tested, but it was not as fast as the laminated board.
for volcano boarding, you have to wear special orange suits so that the ash from the volcano does not harm you.
boarders can reach speeds of up to 60 km per hour.
there is an unofficial record of 97 km per hour.
